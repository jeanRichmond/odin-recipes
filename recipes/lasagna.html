Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish that consists of layered sheets of pasta, typically wide and flat, alternating with various ingredients such as ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, ground meat (usually beef or pork), tomato sauce, and various herbs and spices. The layers are baked together to form a hearty and flavorful casserole.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles (oven-ready or traditional, cooked according to package instructions)
  • Ground beef, ground turkey, or Italian sausage
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning (a blend of oregano, basil, thyme, and rosemary)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Egg (for mixing with ricotta)
  • Fresh basil or parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for added spice)

Steps:

  1. Preheat the Oven.
  2. Cook the Noodles.
  3. Prepare the Meat Sauce.
  4. Prepare the Cheese Mixture
  5. Assemble the Lasagna.
  6. Bake.
  7. Rest and Serve.
