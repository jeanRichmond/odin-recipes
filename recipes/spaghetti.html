Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a type of pasta that is long, thin, and cylindrical in shape. It is one of the most popular and widely consumed pasta varieties worldwide. Spaghetti is typically made from durum wheat semolina or a combination of semolina and water, although variations may include ingredients like eggs.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Salt (for boiling pasta)
  • Water (for boiling pasta)
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Garlic (minced or chopped)
  • Onion (finely chopped)
  • Ground beed or ground Italian Sausage (optional)
  • Italian herbs (such as oregano and basil)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for added spice)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated, for serving)

Steps:

  1. Boil the Pasta
  2. Prepare the Sauce
  3. Cook the Meat (if using)
  4. Add Tomato Sauce
  5. Season the Sauce
  6. Combine Sauce and Pasta
  7. Serve
